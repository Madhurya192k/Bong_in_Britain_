Meals (Thalis)

Veg Thali - £10
A wholesome vegetarian feast featuring a hearty serving of dal, a seasonal vegetable curry, a fried item such as crispy peyaji or bori bhaja, steamed rice, and a delightful dessert like tomator chutney. Perfect for those seeking a balanced and flavorful vegetarian meal.
Katla Thali - £15
A traditional Bengali platter starring a rich Katla Kalia (spicy fish curry with a large piece of Katla), complemented by moong dal, a seasonal vegetable preparation, steamed rice, a fried item, and chutney
Pabda Thali - £14
This flavorful meal centers around Shorshe Pabda (pabda fish cooked in mustard gravy), served alongside a comforting bowl of dal, a mildly spiced vegetable dish, fried boris, steamed rice, and chutney.
Chingri Thali - £14
A seafood lover's delight featuring Chingri Malaikari (prawns in coconut milk gravy), accompanied by fragrant basmati rice, light dal, stir-fried seasonal vegetables, crispy fries or boris, and tangy chutney.
Pomfret Thali - £14
Highlighting the delicious Pomfret fish cooked in a delicately spiced gravy, this platter includes dal, a vibrant vegetable preparation, steamed rice, a crispy side, and tangy chutney.
Mutton Thali - £16
A lavish feast with the star dish being Mutton Kosha (slow-cooked, thick-spiced mutton curry), served with basmati rice, a bowl of comforting dal, a vegetable side, fried potatoes, and tangy chutney.
Moong Daaler Khichuri Thali - £12
A classic rainy-day comfort meal featuring fragrant moong dal khichuri (rice and lentils cooked together with spices), crispy prawn fritters (chingri bora), golden fried potatoes, and tomator chutney.

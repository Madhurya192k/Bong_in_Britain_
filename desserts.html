Desserts and Drinks

Bhapa Sondesh (4) - £6
A luscious, steamed Bengali sweet delicacy made from freshly curdled chhana (cottage cheese) and a hint of jaggery or sugar, offering a soft, melt-in-the-mouth texture and subtle sweetness. Perfect for those who prefer light and refined desserts.
Aam er Shorbot - £4
A refreshing summer drink made with tangy raw mango pulp, spiced with a dash of black salt, sugar, and chilled water, delivering the perfect balance of sweet and sour notes to beat the heat.
Payesh - £5
The quintessential Bengali rice pudding, slow-cooked with aromatic Gobindobhog rice, creamy milk, and fragrant cardamom, sweetened with sugar or jaggery and garnished with nuts for a rich, festive treat.
Shimaier Payesh - £6
A decadent semolina-based milk pudding cooked to a silky consistency, infused with cardamom and occasionally garnished with raisins and cashews for added texture and flavor.
Doi Er Ghol (Lassi) - £3
A refreshing, smooth yogurt-based drink served chilled, lightly sweetened, and perfect for cooling down on a hot day or after a hearty meal.
Soft Drinks - £2
A variety of popular carbonated beverages to accompany your meals.
